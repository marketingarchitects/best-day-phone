**Best Day Phone -- Go-To-Market Business Model Canvas**

**Go-to-Market Business Model Canvas** for the **Best Day Phone**, incorporating both the **healthcare quality** and **consumer-facing** strategies.

**1. Customer Segments**

**Primary Segments:**

- **Family caregivers** of older adults (especially with dementia, isolation, or cognitive decline)

- **Medicare Advantage (MA), D-SNP, and Medicaid MCO plans** under CMS quality pressure

- **PACE programs** and long-term care networks

- **Memory care and assisted living facilities**

**Secondary Segments:**

- Healthcare providers seeking SDOH/CAHPS improvements

- Nonprofit organizations supporting isolated or aging populations

- Technology-forward senior housing developers

**2. Value Propositions**

- **Emotional engagement at scale:** The sound of family, delivered every day

- **Zero-friction setup:** No screens, apps, or passwords

- **Dual-mode access:** Available via direct purchase or payer/facility partnerships

- **Measurable impact:** Improves CAHPS, HEDIS, Stars, SDOH, and caregiver satisfaction

- **Policy-ready platform:** Built for HIPAA, TCPA, and 508 compliance from Day 1

- **Ritual over reminder:** Designed not as assistive tech, but as a source of dignity and belonging

- **Works out of the box:** Cellular-enabled, preloaded with voice message

**3. Channels**

- **Direct-to-Consumer (DTC):**

  - Mass market TV advertising (via Marketing Architects partnership)

  - Website with purchase + gifting + subscription options

  - Social and caregiver influencer campaigns

- **Institutional/B2B:**

  - Medicare Advantage & Medicaid MCO partnerships (Stars, CAHPS, QI teams)

  - State waiver programs (e.g., MLTSS, HCBS)

  - PACE orgs and care facilities via pilot-to-contract model

  - Distribution through DME, ACOs, and home health platforms

**4. Customer Relationships**

- **Consumer:**

  - High-empathy onboarding

  - Emotional storytelling and surprise-and-delight packaging

  - Optional concierge setup for first-time users

- **Institutional:**

  - Dedicated account support for facilities and payers

  - Ritual enablement playbooks and training

  - Pilot-to-scale outcomes dashboards for Stars/HEDIS/CAHPS tracking

**5. Revenue Streams**

- **DTC:**

  - Device sale (\$179--\$199 MSRP)

  - Monthly subscription (\$9.99--\$19.99) for voice library, rituals, and updates

- **Healthcare B2B:**

  - Per-member-per-month (PMPM) licensing (\$5--\$15/month)

  - Facility licensing per device (\$99--\$149/year)

  - White-label licensing for MA plans, ACOs, or nonprofits

  - Pilot fees (fixed short-term deployments)

- **Future Opportunities:**

  - Sponsored voice rituals (e.g., holiday, birthday)

  - Voice memory archive + legacy subscription

  - Analytics-as-a-service for SDOH dashboards

**6. Key Activities**

- Hardware & firmware refinement for pilot-readiness

- National advertising rollout with emotionally resonant storytelling

- Institutional contracting and PBP inclusion support

- Facility pilot deployment and training

- Regulatory alignment and CMS quality metric mapping

- Customer success operations (caregivers and facilities)

- Voice message UX optimization + backend analytics

**7. Key Resources**

- AI voice delivery platform (backend + IVR logic)

- Mass media buying leverage (Marketing Architects partnership)

- Compliance toolkit (HIPAA, TCPA, 508 certified stack)

- Industrial design and pilot-ready hardware supply

- CMS/healthcare policy advisors

- Institutional sales and Stars/CAHPS experts

- Storytelling, brand, and UX design team

**8. Key Partnerships**

- **Marketing Architects** (media buying partner with cost advantage)

- **Healthcare plans** (D-SNPs, MA, PACE orgs)

- **Facilities and LTSS providers** (for pilot scaling)

- **Nonprofits and aging care networks** (for community outreach and equity pilots)

- **Hardware and manufacturing partners** (for scale production)

- **Twilio/Soracom/etc.** (for cellular connectivity)

- **CMS policy consultants & Stars advisors**

**9. Cost Structure**

- Hardware manufacturing and fulfillment (BOM + logistics)

- National TV advertising (efficient spend via MA partner)

- Staff: Product, engineering, customer success, account support

- Compliance/legal + CMS submission support

- Caregiver onboarding and content creation

- Subscription infrastructure (cloud voice storage, playback)

- Institutional sales team + policy/government relations

- Support for pilots, training, outcomes analysis
