# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This document defines the requirements for the Best Day Phone landing page — a marketing website designed to convert caregivers of people with Alzheimer's and dementia into trial subscribers. The page must communicate the product's value proposition, build trust, and drive conversions.

## Glossary

- **Landing_Page**: The marketing website that presents the Best Day Phone product
- **Visitor**: A person viewing the landing page (typically a caregiver researching solutions)
- **Caregiver**: The primary buyer persona — adult children or family members caring for someone with dementia
- **Conversion**: A visitor starting a free trial

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Communicate Value Proposition Immediately

**User Story:** As a caregiver visiting the page, I want to immediately understand what Best Day Phone is and who it's for, so that I can decide if this product is relevant to my situation.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a visitor lands on the page, THE Landing_Page SHALL communicate the core value proposition within 5 seconds of viewing
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL clearly identify the target audience (families caring for loved ones with memory loss)
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL differentiate from generic tech solutions by emphasizing familiarity and simplicity

### Requirement 2: Validate Caregiver Pain Points

**User Story:** As a caregiver, I want to see that the company understands my struggles, so that I feel confident they've built a solution for people like me.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL acknowledge the emotional burden of caregiving (guilt, worry, exhaustion)
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL address why existing technology solutions fail for dementia patients
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL validate that caregivers cannot be present 24/7

### Requirement 3: Explain the Solution

**User Story:** As a caregiver, I want to understand how the product works, so that I can evaluate if it will help my loved one.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL explain the familiar form factor (rotary phone design)
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL explain the AI companion's conversation approach (patient, kind, never corrects)
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL explain how caregivers stay informed about their loved one's wellbeing

### Requirement 4: Address AI Concerns

**User Story:** As a caregiver, I want my concerns about AI addressed honestly, so that I feel comfortable with the technology.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL proactively address skepticism about AI calling a parent
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL position the AI as a supplement to family, not a replacement
3. IF voice cloning is mentioned, THE Landing_Page SHALL present it as optional and secondary

### Requirement 5: Build Trust Through Social Proof

**User Story:** As a caregiver, I want to see evidence that the product works, so that I can trust it will help my family.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL include testimonials or stories demonstrating real usage
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL display key metrics (usage statistics, engagement data)
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL communicate the founder's personal connection to the problem

### Requirement 6: Present Clear Pricing

**User Story:** As a caregiver, I want to understand the pricing options, so that I can choose the right plan for my situation.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL display all pricing tiers with clear differentiation
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL highlight the recommended tier for most families
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL communicate the free device and trial offer prominently
4. THE Landing_Page SHALL address connectivity requirements (WiFi vs 5G) for each tier

### Requirement 7: Answer Common Questions

**User Story:** As a caregiver, I want answers to common questions, so that I can make an informed decision without contacting support.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL address questions about usability for dementia patients
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL address privacy and data concerns
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL address emergency handling capabilities

### Requirement 8: Drive Conversions

**User Story:** As a visitor who is interested, I want clear paths to start a trial, so that I can take action when ready.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL provide multiple opportunities to start a trial throughout the page
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL reinforce low-risk messaging (free trial, cancel anytime, free device)
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL include a final call-to-action for visitors who scroll the entire page

### Requirement 9: Support All Devices

**User Story:** As a visitor on any device, I want the page to be usable, so that I can research and convert regardless of how I access the site.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL be fully functional on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain readability and usability at all viewport sizes

### Requirement 10: Reflect Brand Identity

**User Story:** As a visitor, I want a consistent, warm visual experience, so that I feel the brand is trustworthy and caring.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Landing_Page SHALL use warm, human visual design (not clinical or cold)
2. THE Landing_Page SHALL maintain consistent typography and color throughout
3. THE Landing_Page SHALL support dark mode for accessibility
